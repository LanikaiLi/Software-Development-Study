Faye Wong 王菲

Singer · Songwriter · Actress

Hong Kong singer-songwriter and actress. One of the most influential Cantopop and Mandopop artists of the 1990s and 2000s.

About

Faye Wong (Chinese: 王菲; pinyin: Wáng Fēi; born 8 August 1969 in Beijing) moved to Hong Kong in 1987 and rose to fame with her distinctive voice and eclectic style. She is known for her ethereal tone, fashion influence, and hits in both Cantonese and Mandarin.

Film & Music

She has acted in films such as Chungking Express (1994) and Chinese Odyssey 2002, and released many acclaimed albums including Faye Wong, Restless, and Laughing in the Wind.
